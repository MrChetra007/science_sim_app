
Physics Shot (Wave Lab) - Phase 6: Education Layer
This phase focuses on the "Education Layer" of the roadmap, adding tools that help users understand the underlying physics more deeply.

Proposed Changes
1. Comparison / Ghost Mode
Goal: Allow users to see a "ghost" of the wave before they moved the sliders.
Logic:
Store a WaveState? ghostState in WaveProvider.
Add a "Capture Ghost" button in the 
ControlPanel
.
Update all painters to render the ghostState (if exists) with lower opacity (0.2).
2. Vector Overlays
Goal: Visualize the instantaneous velocity and acceleration of particles.
Visuals:
Small arrows (Vec2) at specific x-intervals.
Green for Velocity ($v_y = A\omega \cos(kx-\omega t)$).
Red for Acceleration ($a_y = -A\omega^2 \sin(kx-\omega t)$).
Painter Update: Add showVectors toggle to 
WaveState
 and draw these vectors in 
WavePainter
.
3. Oscilloscope Graph (fl_chart)
Goal: Show a time-based or position-based graph of a single point.
Implementation:
Create 
OscilloscopePanel
 using fl_chart.
Add to the 
SimulationScreen
 (perhaps as a floating panel or a second tab).
4. Maths Derivation Module
Goal: Provide step-by-step explanations of the formulas.
Implementation:
Add a "Details" button in the 
ResultsPanel
.
Open a DraggableScrollableSheet or a full-screen ModalBottomSheet displaying mathematical breakdowns.
Content categories: "The Equation", "Step-by-Step Derivation", and "Key Variables".
User Review Required
IMPORTANT

Ghost Mode requires UI space for a "Lock/Clear Ghost" toggle. I recommend placing this in the top-right of the Simulation area.

Verification Plan
Toggle vectors and ensure they point in the correct directions (acceleration opposite to displacement).
Verify Ghost wave stays fixed while primary wave changes.
